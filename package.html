Home to the world’s highest and most famous peak. It shouldn’t come as a surprise that Nepal is a sought-after destination for mountain bikers. Even the foothills of the Himalayan range are rocky and gigantic. Sweeping valley views and fantastic perspectives of rugged, snow-capped mountains around every bend. Nepal is simply a superb destination for a trek on two wheels.

A destination where you’re never far from an incredible view, Nepal both awes and humbles visitors with its spectacular landscapes and sincere people. In the home of Everest, mountain biking is indeed some of the best in the world. While the country’s lush, snow-melt fed southern plains are a hidden gem.
